=Update=
==How often will Everything auto update?==
Everything will check for new updates every time it is run.

==How does Everything update?==
When Everything updates it downloads [[/everything/update.ini]]
Once update.ini is downloaded Everything will compare the version information in update.ini with the current version of Everything that you are running.

If the version in update.ini is newer than your version of Everything, Everything will notify you that a new version is available and ask if you would like to download it now.
If you choose to download the newer version, Everything will open up the [[downloads|download page]] where you will be able to download the new version.

==Can I disable auto updating?==
Yes, To disable auto updating open the '''Everything options'''
Click on the '''General''' tab.
Uncheck '''Check for updates automatically''' check box.

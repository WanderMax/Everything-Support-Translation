=Everything=
[[File:support/everything/Everything.Search.Window.png|alt=Everything Search Window]]

"''Everything''" is a filename search engine for Windows.

==How is Everything different from other search engines==
<ul>
* Small installation file.
* Clean and simple user interface.
* Quick file indexing.
* Quick searching.
* Quick startup.
* Minimal resource usage.
* Small database on disk.
* Real-time updating.
</ul>
